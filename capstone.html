Capstone: Game Design Competition

Overall Description

The Game Design Competition is my team's senior capstone project. We are tasked with creating a driving simulator centered around a single-driver vehicle of our choice. The objective is to develop a playable simulation that incorporates Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) features, A.I. traffic tailored to the selected vehicle and setting, and realistic visual elements. For example, the ADAS features might encompass body collision detection and avoidance through Automatic Emergency Braking (AEB), aimed at enhancing a player's performance during gameplay. The A.I. traffic should mimic the real world with physically accurate movements, including smooth braking, accelerating, and turning inputs. To complete the Game Design Competition, we are expected to deliver a demo game that showcases these aforementioned components, providing a comprehensive and immersive driving experience.
